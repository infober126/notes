Dear diary,

Today marks the first entry of my personal blog. I've been meaning to start writing down my thoughts for a while now, and here we are! 🌸

## Why a blog?

I want a place to:
- Capture my daily thoughts and feelings
- Look back on memories years from now
- Write freely without any particular audience in mind

## What to expect

This diary section will hold my personal musings — quiet reflections on daily life, small moments worth remembering, and the occasional long rant about something that's been on my mind.

It won't always be polished. It won't always make sense. But it'll be honest. ✨

Until next time,  
me
