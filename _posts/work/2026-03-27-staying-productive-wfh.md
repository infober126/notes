Working from home has become a permanent fixture in many of our lives. After a few years of trial and error, here are the habits that have genuinely helped me stay productive.

## Morning Routine

Starting the day with intention makes a huge difference:
- Wake up at a consistent time
- Get dressed (even casually) — it signals "work mode" to your brain
- Review your top 3 priorities for the day before opening email

## Workspace Setup

- **Dedicated space**: If possible, designate a specific spot just for work
- **Good lighting**: Natural light boosts mood and focus
- **Minimal clutter**: A tidy desk = a tidy mind

## Time Management

### Time-blocking
Block out chunks of time for deep work, meetings, and breaks. Protect those deep-work blocks fiercely.

### Pomodoro Technique
Work for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break. Repeat. After 4 cycles, take a longer break.

## Avoiding Burnout

- Log off at a consistent time each day
- Take proper lunch breaks (away from your desk!)
- Communicate openly with your team about capacity

The boundary between work and personal life is worth protecting. 🌿
